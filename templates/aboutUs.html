Our Story

Founded in 2018 by tea enthusiasts who grew up enjoying authentic bubble tea in Taiwan, Panda Bubble Tea was born from a simple mission: to bring the genuine taste of traditional Asian bubble tea to our community.

Our journey began when our founders, missing the authentic flavors from their homeland, decided to create a space where everyone could experience the joy of perfectly crafted bubble tea. We spent months perfecting our recipes, sourcing the finest ingredients directly from Taiwan and other Asian countries.

Today, Panda Bubble Tea has become a beloved local destination where tradition meets innovation. We're proud to serve our community with the same passion and dedication that started our journey.
