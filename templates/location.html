How to Reach Us

🚌

By Bus

Take any local bus to Buddha Chowk from Pokhara city center. Our store is easily visible from the main road, just a 2-minute walk from the bus stop.

🚗

By Car/Taxi

Drive towards Buddha Chowk from Prithivi Chowk. Cross the bridge and continue straight - you'll find us on the right side. Parking available nearby.

🚶

Walking

From Lakeside area, it's a pleasant 15-minute walk to Buddha Chowk. Follow the main road towards the city center and look for our panda sign!

🏍️

By Motorcycle

Ride towards Buddha Chowk via the main road. Two-wheeler parking is available right in front of our store for easy access.
